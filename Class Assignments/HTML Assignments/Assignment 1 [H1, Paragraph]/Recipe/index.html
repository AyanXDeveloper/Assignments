Delicious Chicken Biryani Recipe

Chicken Biryani is a popular Indian dish known for its rich flavors and aroma. It is made by layering marinated chicken with basmati rice and slow-cooking it to perfection.

To prepare chicken biryani, first marinate the chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs. Cook partially boiled rice separately. In a heavy pot, layer the chicken and rice, then seal and cook it on low flame using the 'dum' method.

The result is a flavorful and aromatic dish enjoyed by people of all ages. Serve it with raita or salad for a complete meal.
